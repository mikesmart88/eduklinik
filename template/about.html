ABOUT EDUKLINIK

Eduklinik is a learning platform designed to provide educators, administrators and learners with a single robust, secure and integrated system to create personalised learning environments. You can download the software onto your own web server or ask one of our knowledgeable Eduklinik Partners to assist you. Eduklinik is built by theEduklinik project which is led and coordinated byEduklinik HQ, which is financially supported by a network of Eduklinik Partner service companies worldwide.

Eduklinik is a free and open-source learning management system (LMS) developed by Clean code incoporation. It is designed to provide educators, administrators and learners with a single robust, secure and integrated system to create personalized learning environments.Eduklinik is used by millions of users around the world in schools, universities, workplaces and other organizations.

Eduklinik is built on a modular architecture, which allows for easy customization and extension of its features. It supports a wide range of activities and resources, including quizzes, assignments, forums, wikis, databases, and more. Verily still under the process of development some of the aforementioned features will be rolling out in the coming weeks . Eduklinik also has a large community of developers and users who contribute to its ongoing development and improvement.

Built for learning, globally

Designed to support both teaching and learning

With over 10 years of intense knowledge about the ambit of education as a firm and of course guided by social constructionist pedagogy, Eduklinik delivers a powerful set of learner-centric tools and collaborative learning environments that empower both teaching and learning.A lot this features will be rolling out in the next update very soon.

Easy to use

A simple interface and well-documented resources along with ongoing usability improvements make Eduklinik easy to learn and use.

Always up-to-date

The Eduklinik project’s open-source approach means that Eduklinik is continually being reviewed and improved on to suit the current and evolving needs of its users.

Eduklinik in your language

Eduklinik’s multilingual capabilities ensure there are no linguistic limitations to learning online. The Eduklinik community has begun translating Eduklinik into more than 10 languages (and counting) so users can easily localise their Eduklinik site, along with plenty of resources, support and community discussions available in various languages.

Scalable to any size

From a few students to millions of users, Eduklinik can be scaled to support the needs of both small classes and large organisations. Because of its flexibility and scalability, Eduklinik has been adapted for use across education, business, non-profit, government, and community contexts.

Robust, secure and private

Committed to safeguarding data security and user privacy, security controls are constantly being updated and implemented in Eduklinik development processes and software to protect against unauthorised access, data loss and misuse. Eduklinik can be easily deployed on a private secure cloud or server for complete control.

Use any time, anywhere, on any device

Eduklinik is web-based and so can be accessed from anywhere in the world. With a default mobile-compatible interface and cross-browser compatibility, content on the Eduklinik platform is easily accessible and consistent across different web browsers and devices.

Backed by a strong community

The Eduklinik project is well-supported by an active international community, a team of dedicated full-time developers and a network of certified Eduklinik Partners. Driven by open collaboration and great community support, the project continues to achieve rapid bug fixes and improvements, with major new releases every six months. Come explore Eduklinik with us in our community forums!
